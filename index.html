Onepiece Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy is a high spirited guy. He want to become Pirate King as Gol D. Roger(former Pirate King). He is the captain of Straw Hats crew with a bounty of 500 million berries on his head. He ate Gomu-Gomu fruit and became rubber man.

Roronoa Zoro

Zoro is an ambitious guy who wants to become the greates swordsman across the seas. Inspite of using one sword style or two sword style, zoro uses three sword style also called as SANTURYU. He is very strong and have great endurance to pain. He trains himself everyday inorder to become stronger. He is the first mate of luffy. His bounty is 250 million berries.

Catburglar Nami

Nami is a cold hearted culprit. Her dream is to draw world map. She had a very bad childhood becaouse of the angry fishmen tribe. They attacked her village and killed her mother. She was then abducted by them and was made as their worker to draw sea charts for which in return they promised not to hurt anyone in their village. After when she meets luffy, she joins his crew as a navigator.
